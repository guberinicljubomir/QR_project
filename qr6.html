Opis:
U čarobnoj šumi nastaje zbrka kada životinje otkriju da je njihovo omiljeno zvono nestalo! U zajedničkoj potrazi uče koliko su važni prijateljstvo i sloga. Pridružite nam se u ovoj veseloj avanturi punoj smijeha, pjesama i važnih lekcija o timskom radu i saradnji.

ENGLISH VERSION:

In a magical forest, chaos ensues when the animals discover their favorite bell has disappeared! In their joint search, they learn how important friendship and harmony are. Join us in this fun adventure filled with laughter, songs, and important lessons about teamwork and cooperation.
